Last updated: 2026

2. How We Use Data

Collected data is used only to:

3. Data Sharing

OHBump does not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely. Only the bot owner and authorized systems can access stored data.

5. Data Removal

If you want your server or user data removed, you may contact us through our support server.

6. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the bot means you accept the updated policy.
